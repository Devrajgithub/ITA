Play Ground

We have the world's best Play Ground in our polytechnic

Playground is situated within the college campus. Facilities are available to play volleyball, cricket, football, carom, chess, kho-kho, throw ball, boxing and athletics, with a view of developing a sense of team spirit and to encourage a spirit of healthy environment. Opportunity is provided to all students for practice and participant in various sport events in accordance to their interest.
In the academic year 2021-22 our college student won the first and third in district level fencing competition (SDAT) and another student secured first place in divisional and fourth place. Girls secured the fourth places in the inter college chess competition conducted by Itahar General College.
